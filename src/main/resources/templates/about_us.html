About GearBox

Welcome to GearBox, your one-stop online store for all things tech, fashion, and more!

Our Founders

GearBox was founded by two college friends, Ziad and Omar, who shared a passion for technology and entrepreneurship. During their time at university, they noticed a gap in the market for a reliable e-commerce platform that offered a seamless shopping experience for tech enthusiasts and everyday shoppers alike. With Ziad's expertise in software development and Omar's knack for business strategy, they teamed up to create GearBox in 2025, aiming to bring quality products and exceptional customer service to users worldwide.

Our Mission

At GearBox, our mission is to simplify online shopping by offering a curated selection of products across categories like laptops, headphones, mobiles, electronics, toys, and fashion. We strive to provide our customers with the best prices, detailed product information, and a user-friendly platform that makes shopping a breeze.

Our Vision

We envision GearBox as a global leader in e-commerce, known for innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to continuously expand our product offerings, integrate cutting-edge technology, and build a community of happy customers who trust GearBox for all their shopping needs.

Join us on this exciting journey, and let GearBox be your go-to destination for quality products!
